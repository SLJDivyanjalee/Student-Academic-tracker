What's inside

Everything your semester needs, in one place.

No more juggling five different apps. Every LevelUp tool talks to the same schedule and the same data.

Study planner

A study plan that respects your actual life.

LevelUp builds study sessions around your exams, not just your to-do list — while staying inside the working hours and daily limits you set.

  • Exam-aware session generation
  • Custom working hours & daily session caps
  • Locked timetables so a plan doesn't shift under you
Attendance

Know exactly where your attendance stands.

Log lectures as you go and see per-subject percentages update in real time, so a bad week never sneaks up on you before finals.

  • Per-subject attendance percentages
  • Daily log with quick mark-present/absent
  • Streaks that keep you showing up
Progress

See your progress, not just your to-do list.

Charts break down time spent, completion rates and subject-by-subject progress, so you can tell what's actually working before exam week.

  • Subject-by-subject progress bars
  • Weekly study-time charts
  • One glance instead of five spreadsheets
Tasks & assignments

Every deadline, sorted before it sorts you.

Assignments and exams live in one list with priority and due dates, and feed straight into the planner so nothing gets scheduled twice.

  • Assignments and exams in one view
  • Priority and due-date sorting
  • Notifications before things are due

About us

Built by students who were tired of juggling five apps.

LevelUp started as a university project between two computer science students who wanted one dashboard for the parts of student life that actually pile up — timetables, assignments, attendance and study time.

Instead of another to-do list, we built something that understands your exams and your working hours, and plans around both. It's still growing, and every feature you see here came from something we needed ourselves.
